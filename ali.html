About Us

Welcome to Northwest Michigan Therapy Group. We provide compassionate telehealth counseling and therapy services for clients throughout Michigan. Our mission is to support your mental health through video or phone sessions, wherever you are.

Alison R. Hester, MS, LMFT

Pronouns: she/her

Alison R. Hester is a Marriage & Family Therapist exclusively offering Telehealth services. She creates a therapeutic environment based on collaboration, skill-building, action-oriented interventions, and honest, supportive feedback. She is culturally sensitive and respects diversity across a range of cultural contexts.

Specializing in individual, couple, and family therapy with an integrative, systems perspective, Alison believes therapy is a dynamic process that benefits from multiple approaches. She listens for areas where clients may need support, empathy, or insight, and invites them to actively engage in therapy to improve relationships and remove internal/external constraints.

Alison believes every person has resiliency and strengths waiting to be developed to foster growth and a positive sense of self.
