About Me

Welcome to Northwest Michigan Therapist. I provide compassionate telehealth counseling and therapy services to clients throughout Michigan. My mission is to support your mental health through video or phone sessions, wherever you are.

Alison R. Hester, LPC

I am exclusively offering Telehealth services at this time. I create a therapeutic environment based on collaboration, skill-building, action-oriented interventions, and supportive feedback.

Every person has a unique story. I will listen and support your growth, resilience, and self-awareness throughout therapy.

My Services

Individual Therapy

Family Counseling

Couples Therapy

Telehealth Sessions

Convenient and confidential counseling services available via secure video or phone calls anywhere in Michigan.
